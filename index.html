Welcome!

Piano lessons are primarily given in my home in Lunenburg, MA, however special arrangements can be made for me to travel to other locations. I have worked with children of all ages (5-18) as well as adults. Lessons are catered to meet individual needs and preferences of students.

  • Early piano studies in Budapest, Hungary
  • Piano Performance Diploma and Dalcroze Eurhythmics Diploma, Longy School of Music, Cambidge, MA
  • Faculty member at Indian Hill Music, Littleton, MA
  • Student performance opportunities
  • Individualized lessons
  • Diverse styles (classical, contemporary, jazz, pop)
  • Flexible schedule
