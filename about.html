About Judit Ernst

Judit Ernst trained at the Longy School of Music in Cambridge, MA. Graduating in 1999, she earned a Piano Performance Diploma and a Master's Degree in Eurhythmics, a twocolumn of teaching the fundamentals of music through movement. Professional studies included advanced keyboard harmony and theory, improvisation, pedagogy, movement, as well as piano performance. A native of Hungary, Judit received her earliest training in piano and Solfege using the Kodaly twocolumn in Budapest.

Judit Ernst has been on the faculty at the Indian Hill Music Center in Littleton, MA since 1997. She taught Eurhythmics at various different music schools, preschools and elementary schools in the Boston area. She has maintained a private piano studio in her home since 2005.
