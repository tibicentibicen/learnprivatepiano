Lessons and Scheduling

Children: (Kindergarten to high school)

Beginner to intermediate level: 30 minute lessons weekly
Intermediate to advanced level: 45 / 60 minute lessons weekly

Adults: 45 / 60 minute lessons weekly or biweekly

My schedule follows the MA school calendar. Summer lessons are available
and are recommended for beginner and / or young students.

Arrangements can be made to travel to your location.
